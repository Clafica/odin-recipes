Steak with sauteed mushrooms and onions!

The simple steak accompanied by delicous caramilezed onions and browned mushrooms

A simple meal for a simple man.

    Ingredients

  • Choice piece of meat (probably a strip steak)
  • An onion
  • A 8oz package of mushrooms(you can always thinly slice them yourself)
  • a stick of butter
  • 1 handfull of salt

    Instructions

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Pull out your choice piece of steak and rub both sides with salt and let sit. Time to start the grill as well. Preheat to 450ish.
  3. Pull out your mushrooms and go through and clean them. (pull out any odd looking pieces or dirt)
  4. Now throw those mushrooms into a pan on medium heat and add some butter and a pinch of salt. Let the mushrooms brown.
  5. Add onions and allow them to brown up. (once the onions and mushrooms are cooked to your content, cover and let sit)
  6. Take your meat to the grill and throw it on there! Let each side cook about 4-7 minutes. Once each side has been cooked pull it off the grill and let it sit for a few minutes.
  7. Cover your steak withe the sauteed mushrooms and onions. Your meal is Prepared!
